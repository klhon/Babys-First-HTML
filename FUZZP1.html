About What the Hack

Hello! This is Team What the Hack! We are a team of 5 sophomore girls from Stony Brook University. To many's surprise, this is (most of our) first hackathon. Our goal at this hackathon is to simply make a product. We had to scrap our original idea due to its difficulty half way through the hackathon and decided to make a website that showed each of our personalities. Although the product is a simple website, we still faced challenges as it was all of our first time using HTML. This website is the product of our efforts.
